About Ricochet Rumble

A strategic two-player board game that combines elements of chess and tactical shooting. In this game, players take turns moving their pieces across the board, aiming to destroy their opponent's Titan by cleverly maneuvering their pieces and shooting bullets through a series of ricochets.

Cannon: Shoots a bullet after moving.

Richohet: Deflects bullets intuitively in the direction indicated.

Semi-Richohet: Similar to Richohet but deflects bullets only when hit on its inclined part.

Tank: Stops bullets from advancing further.

Titan: The game ends if an enemy's bullet hits it.

INSTRUCTIONS

Each player takes turns moving their pieces one step in any direction.

The Cannon can only move horizontally and must remain in the extreme rows.

Clicking on a piece will display its possible movement locations.

Clicking on a location will move the piece.

After moving the Cannon, it shoots a bullet.

The Recoit and Semi-Recoit pieces can rotate, deflecting bullets in the process. Rotating counts as a move.

Tanks stop bullets from progressing further.

The game ends when a bullet hits the enemy's Titan.
